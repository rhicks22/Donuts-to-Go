Meet the Team


Rocky Rocks, CEO and Founder

Chic culinary artist and confectioner Rocky Rocks, serves as founder, head chef and CEO of Rocky's Sweet Street Cafe. Originally beginning her career as a Assistant Director of Corporate Compliance, Rocky ultimately followed her passion for patries and took her personal and professional dreams in another direction. Rocky believed that delicious patries would be essential to connecting communities and promptly moved to Paris to enroll in culinary school at the world-renowned Le Cordon Bleu Paris.

As head chef and CEO of Rocky's Sweet Street Cafe, Rocky personally writes and creates all of her recipes and trains her select employees in the creation of her high quality desserts – all of which are made from scratch without any preservatives or artificial ingredients. Each day she produces her signature French caramel and chocolate mousse, as well as tailored chocolate blends made from top chocolate producers.

Robby Finance, CFO

I have been a Chief Financial Officer for over 25 years with experience in all aspects of Finance, Treasury, IT and HR for Consumer Products and Technology industry. My experience includes business development planning, financial and cash flow modeling and reporting, contract negotiations, system integration and implementation, raising private equity monies. I have made significant contributions Rocky's Sweet Street Cafe ranging from start-up to learning to assist where needed.

Suzzy Cooken,Pastry Chef

Suzzy Cooken has helped to define what great pastry in America means. After attending culinary school and working within New York restaurants. Suzzy switched to the sweet side of the kitchen and didn’t look back. She was the pastry chef at Stars for eight years.
